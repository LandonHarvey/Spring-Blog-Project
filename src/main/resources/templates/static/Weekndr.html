Website

A working prototype of a travel planning application made utilizing Vue.js, Vuex, and Vuetify. Allows for users to create trips to any destination in the world. Invite friends to join them on there trip. Create itinerary of there trips every step of the way. Hotel, Food and Experience selection. Save photos to individual trip location, like locations and suggestion system to change itinerary. Allows viewers to see their old trips on a map and relieve there experiences. Come take a look at this live, continuously updated application.
